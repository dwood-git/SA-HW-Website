About Us

BrookField Chase Tennis Courts offers a first-class outdoor tennis experience in the heart of Duluth. Our facility features well-maintained blue hard courts surrounded by lush greenery, a welcoming clubhouse, and ample parking. Whether you're here for league play, private lessons, or a friendly match, we provide the perfect setting for players of all skill levels. Our courts are fully wheelchair accessible, ensuring everyone can enjoy the game.
